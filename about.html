about me
Silvia (b. 1999), London-based, with a BA in Fashion Design Womenswear from Central Saint Martins. She moves between fashion, philosophy, and image, treating each as a way of arranging language. Lately she has been making small things in code — quickly, by feel, with the help of language models. This site holds them.
about this building
Morpews was once a gallery of curated experiments. It is now a workshop for vibe-coded products: small programs made quickly, by intuition, and kept anyway. The architecture remains. You enter through the façade, find the plan, choose a door. Nothing here is decorative — the rooms only exist so the work has somewhere to sit.
