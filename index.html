morpews gallery

Morpews Gallery is a digital building made of rooms rather than pages, each screen a wall that listens. Inside, images are cropped, scattered, rearranged; words emerge, dissolve, return. The architecture is quiet — pale stone, dark lines — but alive with invisible labour: algorithms tend the pictures like nocturnal caretakers, and every visitor's gesture rewrites the air. Here, containment and dispersion live side by side: a single, restless mosaic in Glassworks; an ever-expanding firmament in Constellations. Together they form a house where fragments seek their reflections and stories slip, understated, through the seams.
