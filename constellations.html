constellations
Here the wall is divided into many numbered frames. Each frame is fixed in place but empty until uploading an image; on upload, the system assigns that frame a concise motif such as 'horizon' or 'echo'. Visitors may activate any subset of frames in any order. When the 'compose' button is pressed, the algorithm reads the chosen sequence, retrieves the associated motifs, and assembles a brief prose passage that follows the visitor's path exactly. The passage appears once, is not archived, and the wall resets for the next user. The piece treats selection order as syntax and asks whether narrative can emerge from nothing more than a path through static objects.
